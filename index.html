Achievements

TEC Gauntlet Season 1

TEC Gauntlet Season 1 was the first major tournament which was conducted in Indian Valorant. The Squad of Amaterasu,Vibhor,Excali,Antidote,rite2ace thrived in the tourney against teams like Global Esports, Enigma Gaming.

Valorant Conquerors Championship South Asia Season 1

VCC SA Season 1 was the first ever Riot Games official tournament conducted. The Winners were being uplifted with a chance to play against SEA giants of Valorant like Paper Rex, Damwon Gaming, Northeption. Velocity Gaming stood second overall in VCC SA Season 1.

Skyesports Championship 3.0

Skyesports Championship 3.0 is an Indian level tournament organised by esports giant Skyesports. Velocity's Valorant Squad which included hellffR as a new addition stood 1st in the tourney, beating Enigma Gaming in Finals.
